The Philosophy

"Communication through what's already universal."

Protocol % is born from curiosity and experimentation. It's not a commercial product or polished standard — it's a playful exploration of how messages might travel through the universe itself.

Instead of building new infrastructure, we encode meaning into elements already around us. If the medium shifts, the message shape-shifts too — but the values remain intact.

This is about testing boundaries, not solving global problems. Can we build a protocol that uses nature itself as the carrier? Protocol % says yes.
